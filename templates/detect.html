Tips for Best Results

  • Take a clear, well-lit photo of the leaf.
  • Ensure the entire leaf is visible in the frame.
  • Avoid shadows or strong reflections on the leaf surface.
  • If possible, capture both the front and back of the leaf.
  • Choose a leaf that shows symptoms if deficiency is suspected.
  • Avoid using motion-blurred or extremely dark images.
