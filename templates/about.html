About Green Revive Nutrient Fix

Using advanced AI technology to improve plant health and agricultural productivity

The Project

Green Revive Nutrient Fix is an advanced application that uses artificial intelligence to detect nutrient deficiencies in banana and coffee plants. Our system employs deep learning models trained on thousands of leaf images to accurately identify nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium deficiencies.

This project aims to help farmers and gardeners quickly identify nutrient problems in their plants, leading to:

  • Early detection of plant health issues
  • Reduced crop losses due to nutrient deficiencies
  • More targeted and efficient use of fertilizers
  • Improved plant health and crop yields
  • Sustainable agricultural practices
  • Cost savings on unnecessary fertilizers

The Technology

Our application is powered by state-of-the-art deep learning models specifically designed for image recognition and classification tasks:

AI Models

We use a powerful ensemble of MobileNetV3Large and ResNet50V2 models, fine-tuned specifically for plant leaf analysis. These models have been trained on diverse datasets to ensure robust performance across various lighting conditions and image qualities.

Model Architecture

  • Deep convolutional neural networks with attention mechanisms
  • Ensemble learning for improved accuracy and robustness
  • Transfer learning from pre-trained models
  • Optimized for mobile devices with TFLite conversion

Training Process

Our models have been trained using a diverse dataset of leaf images representing various stages of nutrient deficiencies and healthy plants. The training process involved:

  • Data augmentation to improve model generalization
  • Class balancing to handle uneven distribution of deficiency types
  • Fine-tuning of hyperparameters for optimal performance
  • Validation on separate test sets to ensure reliability
